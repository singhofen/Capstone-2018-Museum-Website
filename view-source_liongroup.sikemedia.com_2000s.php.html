<h4>MySpace</h4>
<p>Myspace, LinkedIn, and Skype all launch, attempting to use online communication as their business, allowing for users to instantly send messages to other users, transfer files, and create a home page catered to the user.</p>
<img src="images/myspace.png" class="img-fluid rounded mx-auto d-block" />
<br />
<h4>Facebook</h4>
<p>Facebook launches in 2004, another online communications site, attempting to improve the design of social networking sites, Facebook would become the most popular social networking sites with 2 billion users as of 2017.</p>
<img src="images/facebook.png" class="img-fluid rounded mx-auto d-block" />
<br />
<h4>YouTube</h4>
<p>Youtube launches in 2005, Youtube took a different approach, creating a site focused on video sharing. The site allowed users to upload and maintain a channel, with limited communication tools, mainly a comment section on videos, with focus on communicating through videos. Youtube would flourish in the video sharing market with an estimated 1.3 billion users as of 2018.</p>
<img src="images/youtube.png" class="img-fluid rounded mx-auto d-block" />
<br />
<h4>Twitter</h4>
<p>Twitter launches in 2006, a site that allows for quick snippets of news, or small amounts o text, to be posted for all followers to see. Twitter would gain attention as online communication would improve over the years, attracting actors, directors, community figures and all kinds of people to use its services daily.</p>
<img src="images/twitter.png" class="img-fluid rounded mx-auto d-block" />
<br />
<h4>iPhone</h4>
<p>In 2007 portable phones would have a breakthrough with the release of iPhone. The iPhone brought with it a responsive touch screen and an operating system that would let portable smartphone technology use the Internet fully. All social media outlets including sites like, Facebook, Twitter, Skype and many other applications would become available through smartphones, boosting their position in the current age.</p>
<img src="images/iphone.png" class="img-fluid rounded mx-auto d-block" />
<br />
<h4>iPad</h4>
<p>Following with the trend set by smartphone, Apple release its iPad in 2010. The iPad was design to be a tablet computer, with a much larger screen than smartphones, but with similar touch screens and width. Tablet would continue to improve and become another staple online communications tool.</p>
<img src="images/ipad.png" class="img-fluid rounded mx-auto d-block" />
